📖 User Guide

1 Getting Started

  1. Open the app – works offline and online in any browser (Chrome recommended).
  2. Select a size – choose 15×10cm (Large), 10×6cm (Medium), or 6×4cm (Small) from the top of the sidebar.
  3. Pick a template – Classic, Farm, or Organic visual style.
  4. Choose a colour theme – tap one of the 5 coloured circles to instantly change the sticker palette.
  5. The sticker updates live as you make changes — no need to press any button.

2 Company Info — All Fields

  • Company Name – type your business name; it appears as the big title on every sticker.
  • Contact Number – your phone number, shown with a 📞 icon on the sticker.
  • Tagline / Address – a short slogan, physical address, or any sub-text you want.
  • Email Address – type your email (e.g. info@viroggg.co.ke); it appears with a ✉ icon below the phone number on the sticker. Leave blank to hide it.
  • All fields are fully editable — click any field, clear it, and type your new text.

3 How to Add / Change Email

  1. Look in the left sidebar under the 🏢 Company Info section.
  2. Find the Email Address field (just below Tagline).
  3. Click or tap the field — it will highlight with a pink glow.
  4. Clear the existing email by pressing Backspace / Delete, then type your email address.
  5. The sticker updates instantly — you'll see your email appear on the sticker preview.
  6. To remove the email from the sticker, simply clear the field and leave it empty.
  7. Click 💾 Save Template to remember your email for next time.

4 Production & Expiry Dates

  • Enter Production Date – tap the date picker and choose the packing date.
  • Expiry Date auto-fills exactly 30 days later (Kenya standard shelf life for fresh eggs).
  • You can manually override the expiry date by clicking it and choosing a different date.
  • Add a Batch / Lot Number (e.g. VG-2026-001) for supply chain traceability — shown at the bottom of the sticker.
  • Leave Batch blank and it simply won't appear on the sticker.

5 Printing Your Stickers

  • Go to the 🖨 Print Sheet tab to see the full A4 layout.
  • Choose Quantity per Sheet in the sidebar — 2, 4, 6, or 8 stickers per page.
  • Click 📄 Export PDF to download a PDF you can share via WhatsApp, email, or take to a print shop.
  • Click 🖨 Print Now to send directly to your printer from the browser.
  • Print on A4 sticker paper or regular paper, then use a paper cutter along the dashed lines.

6 Saving & Loading Templates

  • Click 💾 Save Template to store all your current settings (company name, email, phone, theme, size, etc.) in the browser.
  • Click 📂 Load Saved Template to restore your saved settings instantly.
  • This saves locally on your device — no account or internet needed.
  • If you clear your browser data, saved templates will be lost — export a PDF as a backup record.

7 Offline Capability

  • The app is fully functional offline after the first time it loads.
  • All sticker generation, PDF export, and template saving work without internet.
  • The status bar (bottom-right corner) shows a green dot when online, orange when offline.
  • Great for use at the farm or warehouse with limited connectivity.

8 Tips & Troubleshooting

  • Sticker not updating? – Make sure you clicked outside the field or check that JavaScript is enabled in your browser.
  • PDF blank? – Wait a moment after clicking Export; SVG rendering takes 1–2 seconds.
  • Email too long on small sticker? – Use a shorter address or switch to the Large (15×10cm) size.
  • Best browser: Google Chrome on Android or desktop gives the best results.
  • Deploying? Upload the single HTML file to Netlify, GitHub Pages, or Tiiny.host — no server needed.
